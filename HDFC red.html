BrokerBuddy

Android App

HDFC RED is a digital information hub that helps customers through every stage of the house purchasing process. Right from providing detailed information on properties to guiding customers through home loan application processes.

REQUIREMENT

HDFC red "BrokerBuddy" required to be an app specifically targetted to brokers.

Brokers should be able to post requirements in "boards", to which other brokers can then respond. Brokers should also be able to chat with one another, or as a group, to be able to share leads

Design

Challenges

1. Make posting/responding to leads extremely easy.

2. Have a simple and intuitive UI, which will not take time getting accustomed to

3. Support for older android phones

Accessibility

The three main screens that the user will interact most with are all accessible from the home page itself via tabs

This type of layout made it possible to perform almost all the actions on the app via the home screen

Floating action buttons

Inspired by google's material design philosophy, the circular floating action buttons provide quick access to the most important actions.

There are three such buttons, for the three most important actions of the app.
