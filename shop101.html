Shop101

Your own online store

Shop101 is an E-commerce app that allows anyone with a smartphone to be able to set up an online store, complete with order management system, inventory tracking, payment gateways and shipping options to get their business rolling.

Shop101 also allows anyone to browse through thousands of online stores set up by local and national vendors, selling a wide variety of products like handicrafts, jewelry, clothing etc, and make purchases through the app itself.

My job was to research and craft an experience for the shop101 apps (for both Android and iOS, as well as the Web app.)

Browse through 20,000 + sellers across various categories, selling products online ranging from Handicrafts to daily grocieries, all through their website.
