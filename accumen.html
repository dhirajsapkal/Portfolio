Smart + Survey

A case study

Acumen Security is a certification company that simplifies the daunting task of making products compliant to computer security standards. They also offer Gap Analysis to companies that require assistance to identify the development required to make their products compliant.

REQUIREMENT

Acumen Security required a dynamic customer data collection tool that intelligently adapted its questions on the basis of captured responses.

Additionally, they required an admin panel that allowed them to add questions and define conditional flows for the collection process.

Previous Process

Create a list of questions in word

Email the document to customers

Manually read and analyze responses

Copy & Paste reusable questions from previous questionnaires

Design process

Challenges

1. One of our biggest design challenges was how not to overwhelm the respondent with the high number of questions.

2. This leads to respondents spending a fair amount of time on the page. How can we ensure that we don't cause the respondent to deflect in the middle of the process?

3. How does one know how much of the process has been completed/how much is left?

Dark UI theme

The decision to pick a dark UI theme was taken to tackle a single problem. Night time usability

Users taking surveys would do so in sessions, at any time of the day (or night). A darker UI is easier on the eyes when viewed at night, and provides adequate contrast for daytime readability

One page, One question

A single question per page approach where the respondent would have a clear focus on the next step and not be intimidated by the vast number of questions required to be answered.
