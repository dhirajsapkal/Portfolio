A case study

Acumen Security is a certification company that simplifies the daunting task of making products compliant to computer security standards. They also offer Gap Analysis to companies that require assistance to identify the development required to make their products compliant.

REQUIREMENT

Acumen Security required a dynamic customer data collection tool that intelligently adapted its questions on the basis of captured responses.

Additionally, they required an admin panel that allowed them to add questions and define conditional flows for the collection process.

Previous Process
